**"German Throwdown 14.3" scaled**

Mehr Informationen gibt es unter http://www.germanthrowdown.de/.

18 minute AMRAP of:

* 200 m row
* 10 wallball shots 10kg (+5 reps each round)
* 2 (not unbroken) hang snatch 30kg (+ 2 reps each round)

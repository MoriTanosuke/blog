**19.4**

For total time:

3 rounds of:

* 10 snatches
* 12 bar-facing burpees

Then, rest 3 minutes before continuing with:

3 rounds of:

* 10 bar muscle-ups
* 12 bar-facing burpees

Time cap: 12 minutes
